The Problem MIP Solves

What Grand Lodges Need

  • Verify a brother's standing with his home jurisdiction
  • Request Certificates of Good Standing across jurisdictional lines
  • Search for member records when a brother visits or petitions
  • Keep certificates current when a member's standing changes

Today, most of this is done by phone, email, and paper. It's slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

What MIP Is

Open Standard

Published, free, and available to anyone.

Protocol, Not Product

Like the rules that let any email provider talk to any other.

Vendor-Neutral

Any software vendor can implement it.

Direct Connection

Your system talks directly to another jurisdiction's system.

Think of Email: Gmail, Yahoo, and Outlook can communicate because they follow the same rules. MIP does the same thing for Member Data.

What You Can Do with MIP

MIP 1.0 Capabilities

  • Search for members: Cross-jurisdictional search by member number, name, or date of birth.
  • Check standing: Real-time status checks for visiting brothers.
  • Request Certificates: Electronically request and receive Certificates of Good Standing (no paper/phone).
  • Keep certificates current: Revoke or renew certificates automatically when standing changes.
  • Discover the network: Automatically find connected jurisdictions through existing links.

You Control How Requests Are Handled

Every jurisdiction decides for itself.

  • Incoming requests are queued for human review by default.
  • Your office reviews each request and decides what goes out.
  • Jurisdictions can opt into automatic responses for specific functions (e.g., real-time door checks).
  • Nothing leaves your system without your jurisdiction's explicit consent.

Can Work With Every Vendor

You don't change vendors. You don't change systems. Your vendor adds MIP capability.

Security You Can Trust

Every message is protected by the same security used in online banking.

  • Bank-Grade Protection: Every request is digitally signed so it can't be forged or altered in transit.
  • Tamper-Proof: If anyone changes even a single character, the receiving system rejects it automatically.
  • No Middlemen: Data travels directly between the two jurisdictions involved — no third party ever sees it.
  • You Choose Your Partners: Each jurisdiction decides who to connect with and what information to share.

The Network Grows Itself

Web of Trust makes onboarding easier over time.

  • First connections require manual approval (verification by design).
  • When you connect, you exchange cryptographic endorsements.
  • These endorsements can be presented to new jurisdictions as proof of identity.
  • Network Effect: The more connections you have, the easier it is to connect with the next one.

Built for the Future

MIP 1.0 is the foundation. Future versions add capabilities without adding complexity.

Current (1.0)

Search, Standing, Certificates.

Future (Opt-In)

  • Member Linking (formal record connection)
  • Status Change Notifications
  • Opt-in Contact Updates

Every future feature is opt-in. There is no cost to the Grand Lodge; vendors bear the cost.
